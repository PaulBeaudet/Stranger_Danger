About OurService

Say what!?

Our service looks to empower people to connect with strangers in a fast, safe and convienient way, through timed text. Sure, strangers are inherently strange, yet most of our best friends or invaluble professional peers start off an strangers to us. Some of them even remain strange long after becoming aquented! OurService acts a gateway to creating a strong connection, providing a new shared context with people of simiar interest.

Give me friends?

It should be noted that OurService holds no promise of forcing anyone to be your friend. OurService hopes to help create situations where any two users become comfortable with each other enough that they want to continue the conversation regularly IRL ( in real life ) or in more identifing contexts like other networks, phone, or video chat.

How does it work?

It realy easy to get started! Just type any topic that interest you at the moment and ourService shows your topic to similar users or just whoever happens to be on at the time. If the topic peaks someone interest they can select it and WHAMIE BAM! They are in a real time conversation with you. Simple as that. Stop talking to repeat the process, keep talking to potentially make a new friend.

Is OurService Anonomous?

Short answer is yes, the long answer is NO. Asking, anonmous to who? Makes this question more clear. By default, no one needs to use an identy in order to use OurService besides paying to sign-up. Effectively, given two users have never talked before they are in simple terms anonomous to one another. Once users get talking, our paying customers can label users they have had good conversations with. The label basically favorites the person so that if they are on again our robots know to prioritize making the match again.

OurService, does keep track of everyone by a unique ID and stores unique words that they use in order to determine which other users might want to see their topics. OurService strictly uses user data to provide value to the service and its parent companies. Please see our privacy policy for more details about how the data can and cannot be used.
